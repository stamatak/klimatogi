Reforestations 2019 - 2022

Starting in November 2019 and until 2022 we planted trees and other plants obtained from the forestry department on all public areas of Listaros and also installed an extensive irrigation network. Uli and Dorothee kindly assumed the role of coordinating the regular irrigation of the plants.
