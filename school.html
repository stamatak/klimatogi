School Restoration

The current plan is to establish the school as the home of Klimatogi. At present, further actions can not be taken as the school has not yet been transferred to Klimatogi at the land register. We are pushing the municipality toward completeing this transfer. Furthemore, the municipality has stated that EUR 25,000 are available for the school.
