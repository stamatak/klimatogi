Contacts with Municipality

We are in constant exchange with the municipality for various issues, both directly with the guildhall as well as with the president of the communities of Listaros and Kouses.

The municipality/president of Listaros and Kousses have made the following claims (as of March 2020):

  • The garbage bins will be replaced by new ones
  • EUR 25,000 are available for the school
  • The municipality will contribute substantially to village cleaning
  • The wild garbage bins on the road toward the Odigitria monastery will be removed
  • Official recycling will start again in March 2020
  • A request for the renovation of the school has been submitted
  • Progress on cadastral issues is slow but steady
  • Plans for renovation of the village and the construction of a playground will be discussed for the next technical program
  • The issue of derelict buildings in Listaros is being considered
  • The planning for the renovation of the village square and the fountain is in progress
  • The vice-mayor stated in February that he will visit the village

Note that, at present we do not yet have access to the plans for the square and fountain renovation nor to any cadastral plans. We do not know what the EUR 25,000 associated with the school are for. Repeated attempts to set up a meeting the mayor have failed. The vice mayor has not yet visited the village. A request for a video conference to discuss various issues with the president of the communities of Listaros and Kouses has been unanswered since March 18, 2020.

We were informed that other issues such as the construction of a round about on the road to the Odigitria monastery near Sylla's house, the cutting of trees outside Listaros, and the improvement of roads around Listaros fall under the legislation of the region of Crete. Also moving the shepard fence further south to provide free access to Listarokorfi appears to be difficult due to rental contracts with the shepards.

The pictures of the above wild garbage bins on the road toward the Odigitria monastery have been sent to the president of the communities of Listaros and Kousses.
